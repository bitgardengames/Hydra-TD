Hydra TD - Tower Upgrade Modules (Current Names + Descriptions)

Source of truth:
- Branch/tier mapping: world/tower_branch_defs.lua
- Module names/descriptions text: languages/enUS.lua (module + moduleDesc)

Slow Tower
- Tier 2
  - Glacier: Each hit applies strong slow and +50% damage vs slowed enemies.
  - Shardfall: Each hit applies light slow and spawns 1 frost shard.
- Tier 3
  - Snowball: Shots pierce, apply slow, and gain +10% damage per enemy hit.
  - Frost Aura: Hits apply lighter slow and lower direct damage, but emits a high-uptime chill aura around the tower.
- Tier 4
  - Cold Lead: Each hit applies strong slow and +100% damage vs slowed enemies.
  - Whiteout: Each hit applies slow and deals splash damage in a wide radius.
- Tier 5
  - Absolute Zero: Each hit applies near-freeze slow, but deals -25% follow-up damage.
  - Glacial Barrage: Hits deal lower direct damage, but striking already-slowed enemies occasionally triggers a burst cleave of frost shards.

Lancer Tower
- Tier 2
  - Overdrive: Every 4th hit fires an overdrive shot for +140% damage.
  - Splitfire: Each hit fires 2 split follow-up shots.
- Tier 3
  - Overpenetration: Projectiles pass through enemies without being consumed.
  - Ricochet: Shots bounce to a nearby enemy after impact.
- Tier 4
  - Sustained Barrage: Fires in 6-shot cycles; shots 1-3 deal +35% damage.
  - First Blood: Hits deal +90% damage to enemies above 80% HP.
- Tier 5
  - Rail: Shots use linear travel and gain +2 pierce.
  - Focus Fire: Repeated hits on the same target gain +12% damage per stack.

Poison Tower
- Tier 2
  - Blight: Applies strong poison with -3 max stacks.
  - Plague Cloud: Applies weaker poison per stack, but +6 max stacks.
- Tier 3
  - Neuro: Applies steady poison and adds +1 extra stack per hit.
  - Venomburst: On poisoned enemy death, poison spreads to nearby enemies.
- Tier 4
  - Envenom: Hits deal +6% direct damage per poison stack on target.
  - Spillover: Each hit splashes poison to up to 2 nearby enemies.
- Tier 5
  - Hemotoxin: Poison damage gains up to +60% based on target missing HP.
  - Pandemic: On poisoned enemy death, poison spreads and can chain 1 more time.

Cannon Tower
- Tier 2
  - Siege: Fires fewer heavy shells with +40% impact damage and larger blasts.
  - Barrage: Fires faster light shells for higher sustained splash damage.
- Tier 3
  - Cluster: Shells split into 3 smaller shells on impact.
  - Shockwave: Impacts launch enemies, then emit 1 damaging shockwave.
- Tier 4
  - Long Fuse: Impacts deal normal blast damage, then trigger 1 larger delayed blast.
  - Frontline: Impacts burst into 3 short-range follow-up shells.
- Tier 5
  - Megashell: Fires massive shells with +60% damage and huge blast radius.
  - Carpet Fire: Fires lighter shells with 2 delayed follow-up blasts.

Shock Tower
- Tier 2
  - Stormcoil: Chain lightning gains +3 jumps.
  - Overcharge: Chain lightning has -2 jumps but +35% damage kept per jump.
- Tier 3
  - Fork Arc: Chain lightning forks 1 side arc on each jump.
  - Static: Repeated hits on the same target gain +10% surge damage per stack.
- Tier 4
  - Crowdhunt: Chain lightning gains +2 jumps and stronger side arcs.
  - Kingslayer: Repeated hits gain stronger surge damage and endpoint mini-bursts.
- Tier 5
  - Thunderstorm: Chain lightning gains long reach and extra side arcs.
  - Meltdown: Chain endpoints detonate for extra splash damage.

Plasma Tower
- Tier 2
  - Lance: Fires a focused plasma bolt that burns in a tight area.
  - Unstable: Fires an unstable plasma bolt that burns a wide area over time.
- Tier 3
  - Boomerang Shot: Plasma travels out, then returns to the tower.
  - Spiral Drive: Plasma travels in a spiral path.
- Tier 4
  - Solar Lance: Extends plasma reach with a narrow burn trail.
  - Corona Bloom: Turns plasma into a volatile bloom that burns a wider area faster.
- Tier 5
  - Supernova: Plasma burns while traveling, then detonates near the end for a heavy burst.
  - Starforge: Plasma grows as it travels, increasing area coverage over time.
