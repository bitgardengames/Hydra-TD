Hydra TD - Tower Upgrade Modules (Name + Effect)

Source of truth:
- Branch/tier mapping: world/tower_branch_defs.lua
- Module names/effects text: languages/enUS.lua (module + moduleDesc)

Slow Tower
- Tier 2
  - Glacier: Each hit heavily chills enemies for a long duration and deals bonus damage to already slowed targets.
  - Shardfall: Each hit applies a light chill and spawns one shard shot to boost coverage and total hits.
- Tier 3
  - Shatter: Each hit applies a steady chill and deals extra damage to already slowed enemies.
  - Snowball: Shots pierce through enemies, ramp up damage as they pass through crowds, and apply chill on hit.
- Tier 4
  - Cold Lead: Each hit applies a heavy chill and gains major bonus damage against already slowed enemies.
  - Whiteout: Each hit applies chill and deals splash damage in a wider area.
- Tier 5
  - Absolute Zero: Each hit almost stops enemies; excels at hard control with modest follow-through damage.
  - Hailstorm: Each hit applies a lighter chill and bursts into dense shard volleys for sustained crowd damage.

Lancer Tower
- Tier 2
  - Overdrive: Every 4th hit fires an overdrive round for +140% bonus damage.
  - Splitfire: Each hit releases 2 follow-up split shots.
- Tier 3
  - Overpenetration: Projectiles pass through enemies without being consumed.
  - Ricochet: Shots bounce to a nearby enemy after impact.
- Tier 4
  - Sustained Barrage: Fires in repeating burst cycles: the first 3 shots in every 6-shot cycle deal bonus damage.
  - First Blood: Hits deal heavy bonus damage to enemies above 80% HP.
- Tier 5
  - Rail: Shots switch to linear movement and gain pierce.
  - Focus Fire: Repeated hits on the same target within a short window build bonus rifle damage stacks.

Poison Tower
- Tier 2
  - Blight: Applies strong poison damage with a lower stack cap.
  - Plague Cloud: Applies weaker poison per stack, but allows many more stacks.
- Tier 3
  - Neuro: Applies steady poison and adds an extra stack with each hit.
  - Venomburst: When a poisoned enemy dies, poison spreads to nearby enemies.
- Tier 4
  - Envenom: Hits deal bonus direct damage based on poison stacks on the target.
  - Spillover: Each hit splashes poison onto up to 2 nearby enemies, spreading stacks and duration.
- Tier 5
  - Hemotoxin: Poison damage scales with target missing health.
  - Pandemic: When a poisoned enemy dies, poison spreads to nearby enemies and can keep chaining through new kills.

Cannon Tower
- Tier 2
  - Siege: Fires fewer, heavier shells with larger blast zones and higher direct impact damage.
  - Barrage: Fires faster, lighter shells for stronger sustained splash throughput in clustered waves.
- Tier 3
  - Cluster: Shells split into 3 smaller bomblets on impact.
  - Shockwave: Blast impact launches enemies, then emits a damaging radial shockwave for heavy two-stage AoE bursts.
- Tier 4
  - Long Fuse: Impact deals normal blast damage, then after a short delay detonates a larger secondary blast with a bonus-damage ring.
  - Frontline: Impact bursts into 3 short-range follow-up shells, trading per-hit power for stronger close-pack sustained splash.
- Tier 5
  - Megashell: Fires massive high-damage shells with a huge blast radius.
  - Carpet Fire: Fires lighter shells that follow up with 2 delayed blasts.

Shock Tower
- Tier 2
  - Stormcoil: Chain lightning jumps through many nearby enemies.
  - Overcharge: Chain lightning makes fewer jumps but keeps more of its damage per jump.
- Tier 3
  - Fork Arc: Chain lightning jumps between enemies and forks into extra side arcs.
  - Static: Repeated hits on the same target build stacking surge damage.
- Tier 4
  - Crowdhunt: Adds several extra chain jumps and stronger side-forks to heavily boost crowd damage output.
  - Kingslayer: Amplifies repeated-hit surge damage and triggers small endpoint bursts for stronger heavy-target DPS.
- Tier 5
  - Thunderstorm: Greatly extends chain reach and adds side-arcs for maximum large-pack damage output.
  - Meltdown: Chain endpoints detonate for extra area damage.

Plasma Tower
- Tier 2
  - Lance: Fires a focused plasma bolt that burns enemies in a tight area as it travels.
  - Unstable: Fires a less stable plasma bolt that burns a wider area over time.
- Tier 3
  - Boomerang Shot: Plasma travels out, then returns.
  - Spiral Drive: Plasma follows a spiral path.
- Tier 4
  - Solar Lance: Extends plasma reach with a narrow, high-pressure burn trail for stronger long-lane damage.
  - Corona Bloom: Compresses plasma into a volatile bloom that burns a much wider area at a faster tick rate.
- Tier 5
  - Supernova: Plasma burns while traveling, then detonates near the end of its life for a heavy burst.
  - Starforge: Plasma grows larger as it travels, increasing its area coverage over time.
